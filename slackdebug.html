TMufasaBitmap.DrawSkillReport¶

function TMufasaBitmap.DrawSkillReport(At: TPoint; Color:Int32; CurrentXP, GainedXP, Objectives: Int32; Skill:String=''): TBox;

Draws a skill report at the given location. Color can be just about any color, it will be adjusted automatically to look as good as possible.

You have to track your XP, gained XP, and how many “objects” you have complted yourself in your script. This is only for debugging that.
